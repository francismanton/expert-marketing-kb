TITLE: The KPIs You Must Track To Succeed With Challenge Funnels
VIDEO: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5dMSP2xWhyg
CATEGORY: Funnels - Challenge
============================================================

CORE CONCEPT:
Free and paid challenge funnels have dramatically different KPI benchmarks. Paid challenges ($47-$97 tickets) deliver 70% Day 1 show rates, 60-65% Day 2, 50% Day 3, and 3-7% conversion rates against total buyers. Free challenges see 10-20% show rates with 50% daily drop-off but can work at massive scale (150K+ registrants) with 1-3% conversion rates.

TACTICAL TAKEAWAYS:
- Paid challenge benchmarks: 5-15% sales page conversion rate for ticket purchases, 70%+ Day 1 show rate, 60-65% Day 2 (when you make your pitch), 50% Day 3 for the rep pitch. Average conversion rate to high-ticket offer is 3-7% of total ticket buyers (not just attendees), with top performers hitting 10%.
- Ticket pricing strategy: $47-$97 produces more qualified buyer leads who convert at higher rates to high-ticket ($9K-$15K+ offers). $7-$9 tickets can achieve the same show rates but attract less qualified leads. Price selection should be based on what you're ultimately selling and your profitability target going into the event.
- Maximize AOV with 2 upsells that directly complement the challenge theme — like fries complementing a burger. Upsell take rates of 20-50% are achievable when the offer is complementary and priced right. Lower price = higher take rate. The AOV must exceed cost per purchase for profitability going in.
- Cost per purchase is driven by CPMs, link click-through rate, page conversion rate, and upsell take rates. If unprofitable going in, run a bottleneck analysis on each specific stat rather than abandoning the strategy. The webinar promotion approach adds opt-in rate and webinar show rate as additional variables.
- Free challenges: Expect 1-3% conversion rate against total registrants, massive daily attendance drop-off, and susceptibility to gaming (bribes like raffles attract show-up-and-leave behavior). Only makes sense when you can drive 100K+ registrants through massive organic reach.

KEY QUOTES:
- "If you're profitable just on ticket sales and the upsells on the front end, you have unlimited budget."
- "It's a combination of these two things together that matter the most — the price and it being complementary to whatever the main thing is."

APPLY WHEN:
You're planning or currently running a challenge funnel and need specific KPI benchmarks to evaluate performance. Use these numbers to set expectations, identify bottleneck statistics, and decide between free vs. paid and long-form sales page vs. webinar promotion strategies.
