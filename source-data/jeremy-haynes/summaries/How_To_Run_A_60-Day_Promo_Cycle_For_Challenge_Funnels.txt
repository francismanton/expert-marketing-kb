TITLE: How To Run A 60-Day Promo Cycle For Challenge Funnels
VIDEO: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-G7AFPzL5I8
CATEGORY: Funnels - Challenge
============================================================

CORE CONCEPT:
Challenge funnels and 3-day virtual events are lump-sum funnels that require a structured 60-day promotional cycle with escalating pricing tiers, aggressive ad creative volume for Andromeda compliance, and a strategy of reinvesting every profitable dollar back into ad spend to maximize event attendance.

TACTICAL TAKEAWAYS:
- Structure the 60-day promo cycle into three pricing tiers: Early Bird (weeks 1-4, $37-$47 general / $97-$150 VIP), Extended Early Bird (weeks 5-7, $87-$97 general / $150-$200 VIP), and Punisher pricing (final week, ~$147 general / $200-$250 VIP). Show the upcoming price crossed out at each tier to build trust.
- When transitioning from Early Bird to Extended Early Bird pricing, open new cold audiences simultaneously so fresh prospects don't have anchor bias to the lower price — this dramatically reduces the conversion dip during price transitions.
- Prepare minimum 25-30 unique video creatives for Andromeda compliance, each with 5 body copy variations and 5 headlines. At higher spend levels, you'll need even more. These can be multiplied with B-roll combinations and clip mixing.
- Maximize AOV through VIP ticket upsells (target 30-50% VIP take rate), checkout page order bumps ($10-$30 add-ons), two post-checkout one-click upsell pages, and a call funnel on the confirmation page to close high-ticket before the event starts.
- Reinvest every dollar of ticket revenue back into ad spend — the goal is to be ROI-positive on ticket sales going into the event, then the actual event upsell becomes pure additional profit. Scale aggressively using cost cap bidding when cost per purchase allows it.

KEY QUOTES:
- "These are your gambling dollars. Your goal should be spend whatever initial ad spend, get that money back in real time, and literally every dollar you get back, throw right back into the total ad spend pile."
- "We've scaled from 10k a day to 25k a day to 50k a day to 75k a day through cost cap bidding."

APPLY WHEN:
You're planning a challenge funnel or 3-day virtual event and need to structure the promotional timeline, pricing escalation, and ad spend strategy. Essential for anyone about to commit $100K+ in ad spend to a lump-sum event funnel and wanting a proven promotional framework.
