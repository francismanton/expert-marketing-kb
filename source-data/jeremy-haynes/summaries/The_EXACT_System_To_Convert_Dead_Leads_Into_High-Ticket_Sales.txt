TITLE: The EXACT System To Convert Dead Leads Into High-Ticket Sales
VIDEO: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hBgKiEC_Afc
CATEGORY: Sales
============================================================

CORE CONCEPT:
The "group closing call" is a weekly (or twice-weekly) Zoom session where the business owner or authority figure personally re-engages all unclosed leads from the sales team, leveraging celebrity authority and group dynamics to convert deals that individual sales reps couldn't close.

TACTICAL TAKEAWAYS:
- Calculate your unclosed rate (inverse of close rate) and convert it to a weekly number. If 100 leads go unclosed per week, 50 agree to attend the group call, 25 actually show up, and you close 20% — that's 5 additional deals per week at your AOV, often worth tens of thousands per hour of the owner's time.
- Best days for group closing calls are Tuesdays and Fridays. Salespeople pitch the call in real-time when a lead doesn't close: "You can get on a group call with [owner name] later this week to ask questions directly." This works because authority transfer from founder to sales rep is always diluted.
- Structure is simple: CRM tag triggers automated registration, Zoom calendar invite, and follow-up sequence. Format is part coaching, part Q&A, part pitch — similar to a webinar but more direct since attendees already had a sales conversation.
- Zoom private messaging allows shy attendees to submit questions anonymously to the host, removing the friction of asking in front of others. This dramatically increases engagement from people who wouldn't unmute on a group call.
- Pay the original sales rep a reduced commission (setter-level, not full closer commission) for pushing leads to the group call. This incentivizes reps to make the handoff while keeping economics favorable. Combine with a "cleaner" role that follows up with all unclosed leads and pushes them toward both the group call and individual re-engagement.

KEY QUOTES:
- "75% of those people are not closing and giving you some cash. That issue in itself is worth however much you can quantify it being worth."
- "The celebrity authority effect makes a tremendous difference to the volume of people that are probable to close during these weekly group calls."

APPLY WHEN:
You have a personal brand or recognized authority figure in your business, your sales team has full calendars but close rates below 30-40%, and a high volume of unclosed leads are going unfollowed. This is especially effective when the authority gap between your brand and your sales reps is significant.
