TITLE: I Helped These 8 Businesses Hit $1M/Month, Here's What Actually Works
VIDEO: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WNmKQO-3YCU
CATEGORY: Case Studies
============================================================

CORE CONCEPT:
Eight businesses that cracked million-dollar months share common patterns: webinars dominated as the primary funnel type, sales team quality was the most frequent bottleneck, and many members' biggest breakthrough was simply a mindset shift from being exposed to peers operating at higher levels — not a tactical change.

TACTICAL TAKEAWAYS:
- Webinar iteration frequency determines speed to profitability. Stamar ran webinars consistently for ~8 months, eating losses on many, until enough iterations compounded into profitable, scalable events. Daily data funnels (call funnels, DM ads) iterate faster than weekly webinars.
- Sales team quality is the single highest-impact variable. Josh's revenue dropped from $700-800K to $400K when switching from a non-compliant sales agency to a new team, endured a 3-month acclamation period, then built an in-house team that immediately pushed ROAS back to 4x and cracked $1M/month.
- Ed went from "I don't think a million a month is possible for my niche" to $1M/month in profit by solving one problem: recruiting, training, and managing closers. He stopped closing all deals himself after recognizing his "specialized knowledge" belief was the bottleneck. His top closer eventually produced $200K+ in monthly cash collected.
- Multiple winners (Devon, Jesse, Brandon) had all their metrics already performing well — their breakthrough was purely exposure to peers taking bigger action, triggering a mindset shift that made them scale aggressively.
- Edward went from $30K/month to $500K/month using the Master Internet Marketing program alone, then cracked $1M/month within ~2 months of joining Inner Circle after financial modeling revealed he wasn't reinvesting enough.

KEY QUOTES:
- "Nothing really changed when it came to all the front-end stuff. The main thing that made the biggest difference was the sales team."
- "They see all these other people hitting bigger numbers, realize all their numbers were already good, and just press the gas."
- "He went from 'I don't think it's possible' to 'I cleared a million bucks a month in profit.'"

APPLY WHEN:
You're stuck at $300K-$800K/month and trying to identify what's actually holding you back. Use these case studies to diagnose whether your bottleneck is funnel iteration frequency, sales team quality, mindset limitations around scaling, or simply not pressing hard enough on what's already working.
