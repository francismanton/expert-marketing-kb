TITLE: The ONLY System You Need To Get 80%+ Of People To Show Up
VIDEO: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z5LG8QtpBTs
CATEGORY: Show Rates
============================================================

CORE CONCEPT:
Show rate improvement requires stacking all four back-end selling systems (confirmation page, value-dense emails, setter outreach, Hammer Them campaigns) and iterating messaging within each until you find the specific angle that produces double-digit lifts — most people gaslight themselves into thinking they've tried everything when they've barely done 10%.

TACTICAL TAKEAWAYS:
- Confirmation page breakout videos answering common sales questions may only lift show rate a few percentage points, but a single urgency-based video with highly believable, real-world reasons to prioritize the call/webinar delivered a 14% lift for one client. The key is finding the RIGHT messaging, not just adding more videos — one client had 19 breakout videos before the 20th (urgency-based) created the breakthrough.
- Value-dense email sequences: Take messaging that works on the confirmation page and roll it into emails. Send actual valuable content IN the email body (not click-through links — click rates are single digits). Start at 3/day, scale to 6/day as long as open rates hold. Subject lines matter, but content value determines whether people unsubscribe or engage.
- Setter outreach: Assume the show-up (like a Five Seasons concierge), don't selfishly confirm attendance. Send personalized selfie videos, tailor specific resources based on application data, find similar testimonials for their situation. This turns education calls into sales conversations.
- Hammer Them campaign: Distribute 30-50 short-form and 15-30 long-form content pieces to booked leads via paid ads on social platforms, targeting a frequency of 15-20 impressions between booking and call/webinar. Pick strategic content — not random pieces, but content that builds trust, handles objections, and frames properly.
- Stop gaslighting yourself: Take actions until they work, not once and quit. Rotate messaging across all four systems until you find what your specific demographic actually responds to. If play rates and engagement rates are high but show rate doesn't lift, it's the wrong messaging, not a broken system.

KEY QUOTES:
- "You took the action until it works because it works. You don't take it once and then gaslight yourself into saying it didn't work."
- "What's a 20 to 30% lift in your show rate actually worth? How many dollars would you generate as a result of that lift?"

APPLY WHEN:
Your show rate is stagnant despite having tried "everything" — this framework forces you to systematically stack all four back-end selling systems and iterate messaging within each until you find the specific angles that produce double-digit lifts for your particular demographic.
