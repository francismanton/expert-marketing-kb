TITLE: How To Plan For $1M Months In 2026 (Full Year Roadmap)
VIDEO: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WTrl_dZsLBM
CATEGORY: Strategy
============================================================

CORE CONCEPT:
Million-dollar months come from running multiple funnel types simultaneously on a structured annual calendar: daily lead flow (call funnels, low-ticket-to-high-ticket, DM ads), weekly cold webinars + monthly warm webinars as one-off revenue drivers, and quarterly 3-day virtual events/challenge funnels as lump-sum revenue events — all planned out for the entire year in advance.

TACTICAL TAKEAWAYS:
- Start the year with a 3-day virtual event in February: promote with a 60-day cycle starting in December/January. People are still motivated by new goals and self-improvement. These events typically generate multi-million dollar lump sums from ticket sales plus high-ticket offers ($10K-$50K). Repeat quarterly throughout the year. Short promotion cycles produce ~$200K events; 60-day cycles produce multi-million dollar events.
- Run daily lead flow year-round through a combination of: call funnels (dominant revenue driver for most), low-ticket-to-high-ticket funnels (with setter activation and closer upsell), and DM ads (supplemental "chunk method" — contributes to the million but rarely hits it alone). Most million-dollar months come from having multiple funnels running simultaneously.
- Webinar schedule: weekly cold-audience live webinars + once-monthly warm-audience webinar. Cold first because it's harder — mastering cold makes warm webinars feel like layups. Never run automated/evergreen webinars; live dramatically outperforms in current market. Warm audience webinars are capped at monthly to prevent fatigue.
- Seasonal strategy: January — crank ad spend after Jan 3-5 when everyone's back and motivated. March through September is the prime scaling window (shoot the gap). Q4: testing is unreliable due to auction inflation, pull back around Thanksgiving/Christmas, frontload December spend early. European markets have a shorter window (March-June) due to August summer disengagement.
- Fund growth through reinvestment: take a percentage of ROI and siphon it into increased ad spend. Scale messaging by testing variations — Q4 test results are inconclusive, so re-test in Q1 when costs normalize. When funnels plateau, launch new funnel types or prioritize scaling a different existing funnel rather than forcing more spend into a stalled one.

KEY QUOTES:
- "Rather than just having one funnel, this is where they have a lot of funnels — call funnels, low-ticket-to-high-ticket, DM ads, live webinars all running simultaneously."
- "The absolute dogs do cold webinars at the highest frequency, dial them in, then crank down the difficulty for the warm audience webinar — it's like a Kobe-to-Shaq alley-oop."
- "Whatever tests you run during Q4 are generally inconclusive — go back and re-test in Q1 when CPMs drop and people are back and ready to buy."

APPLY WHEN:
Planning annual marketing strategy and revenue targets, deciding which funnel types to add to your current mix, or structuring a year-long promotional calendar. Essential for businesses already doing $200K-$500K/month looking to break through to $1M/month by diversifying funnel types and scaling multiple revenue channels simultaneously.
