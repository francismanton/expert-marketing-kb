TITLE: How To Build Webinar Funnels That Consistently Convert High-Ticket Clients
VIDEO: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IiUSTYpidJA
CATEGORY: Funnels - Webinar
============================================================

CORE CONCEPT:
Profitable webinar funnels require rigorous financial modeling with worst-case-scenario statistics before spending a dollar, a systematic asset creation and promotion process, layered back-end selling systems to maximize show rates and close rates, and disciplined bottleneck analysis post-webinar to identify and fix the specific stat that's contracted.

TACTICAL TAKEAWAYS:
- Start every webinar with a financial model using worst-case statistics: $30 CPL (conservative), 10-15% show rate for ice-cold first-time webinars (30% once dialed in), 10-15% call booking rate initially (30% baseline when polished), 50-60% call show rate (70% norm), and minimum 20-30% close rate. Know exactly how bad each stat can be before you lose money.
- For your first webinar, promote 10-14 days out to give yourself time to fix issues. Once dialed in, compress to a 3-5 day promotion cycle — leads who opt in closer to the webinar date have dramatically higher show rates (day-of opt-ins show at the highest rate).
- Launch with 25-30 video ads and 25-30 image ads to follow Andromeda best practices regardless of budget. Pair individual ads with dynamic copy (5 headlines, 5 primary text variations). Use standard talking head with jump cuts and captions for video; use webinar topic/headline for image ads.
- Layer all four back-end selling systems: (1) Hammer Them strategy — 30-50 short-form and 15-30 long-form content pieces retargeted to opt-ins, (2) dialed-in confirmation pages with breakout videos and testimonials, (3) value-dense email sequences (not just reminders), and (4) setter outreach to improve show rates. Do NOT send the Zoom link via text — this increased phone attendance which tanked booking rates.
- Do live webinars only — automated webinars are dramatically underperforming in direct comparison. Aim for 80% retention from peak show rate to time of pitch. Bait attendees to stay through the pitch with bonuses sent immediately after. Post-webinar, send the replay with embedded application to ALL opt-ins (showed up or not).

KEY QUOTES:
- "It's not random when a webinar doesn't work. It's a very specific bottleneck — there's some specific statistic within your KPIs that is contracted."
- "I would fire the shit out of a closer if they got less than a 30% close rate from webinar leads. Webinar leads are layup deals."
- "Hope is not a strategy — if circumstances are bad enough for you to seek this out, don't deploy hope as the tactic."

APPLY WHEN:
Launching a webinar funnel for the first time and need a systematic process, or when an existing webinar is underperforming and you need to diagnose whether the issue is in promotion, show rates, the webinar itself, or the sales process. Critical for high-ticket offers ($3K+) using a book-a-call model.
